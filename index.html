How did it all start?

The Start

Pathfinder began as a class project at BCIT. Our team saw how hard it can be to choose a direction after high school or during college, so we wanted to build a small tool that makes exploring options a bit easier.

Our Goal

Our goal isn't to tell you exactly what to do, but to make it simpler to see what's possible. We want to help people connect their interests, strengths, and values with real programs and career paths, and feel more confident about their next step.

How we'll do it

Pathfinder uses a short personalization survey to suggest careers and study options that might match you. From there, you can:

  • browse information about different roles and programs
  • see typical tasks, skills, and education for each path
  • use tools on the site to collect ideas and build a rough plan

It's designed to be a starting point you can bring into conversations with advisors, friends, or professionals.
